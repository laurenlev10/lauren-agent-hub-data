Privacy Policy

MBS Automation · The Makeup Blowout Sale Group Inc. · Effective June 04, 2026

MBS Automation ("the App") is an internal tool operated by The Makeup Blowout Sale Group Inc. ("we", "us"). It connects to our own QuickBooks Online company to read accounting data and produce internal post-event profit-and-loss summaries for our retail events. The App is used only by our company and is not offered to or used by third parties.

What data we access

Through the QuickBooks Online API we read accounting records from our own company file — expense, purchase, bill and check transactions and their associated Class, account, vendor, date and amount. We do not access data belonging to any other QuickBooks company.

How we use it

The data is used solely to calculate internal event profit-and-loss figures (e.g. travel, venue and other non-cash expenses by event). We do not sell, rent, or share QuickBooks data with any third party, and we do not use it for advertising.

Storage & security

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored as encrypted secrets and are accessible only to our internal automation. Financial figures are used transiently to generate summary reports. Access is limited to the company owner and authorized staff.

Data retention & deletion

You may disconnect the App at any time from within QuickBooks (Apps → Connected apps), which revokes its access. On disconnection we delete the stored tokens. To request deletion of any stored summary data, contact us below.
